Functional Requirements

1.Users can search restaurants by name, cuisine, or location.
2.Users can view menu items.
3.Users can add items to cart.
4.Users can place orders.
5.Users can track order status.
6.Restaurants can manage menus and accept orders.
7.Delivery partners can pick up and deliver orders.
8.Users can make payments (UPI, card, wallet, COD).
